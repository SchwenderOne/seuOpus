# Visual Style

## Aesthetic
- **Pixel Art**: Clean, readable pixel art style.
- **Theme**: Dark Fantasy / Dungeon Crawler.
- **Perspective**: Top-down (orthographic or slight 3/4 tilt).

## Color Palette
- **Environment**: Earthy tones (browns, greys, greens) for dungeons and nature.
- **Characters**: Distinct colors to separate player from background.
- **UI**: Dark panels with light borders (gold/silver) for high contrast.

## Technical Details
- **Base Resolution**: Assets are designed for a 32px grid.
- **Scaling**: Recommended 2x or 3x scale for modern 1080p+ displays.
- **Animation**: Frame-by-frame sprites (4-6 frames typical).
